Manage your library card

Pay a fine

Didn't return an item on time? Learn about fines on overdue materials, including payment methods.

Forgot your PIN?

Reset your four-digit PIN via email or visit a location near you.

Renew your card

Every cardholder must renew his or her library card every three years. Find out how.

Replace a lost or stolen card

If your card is lost or stolen, report it immediately. You can email, call us, or visit us in person.
